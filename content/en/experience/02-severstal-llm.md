- **Corporate Voice Multi-Agent System**:
  - **Situation/Task**: Design and build an agentic system that safely orchestrates calendar management, email retrieval, and RAG search through speech.
  - **Action**: Architected a Hierarchical Orchestration model (Planning → Executor) on OpenAI Agents SDK (GPT-5.2, Claude 4.6 Opus) with Next.js + WebRTC; integrated a Graph-RAG pipeline (tokenization, embeddings, retrieval, reranker) and FastMCP connectors (Gmail, Google Calendar, Perplexity Sonar API).
  - **Result**: Delivered reliable agent workflows — controlled tool-call execution, knowledge-grounded answers, structured actions, and observable agent state over real-time SSE.
